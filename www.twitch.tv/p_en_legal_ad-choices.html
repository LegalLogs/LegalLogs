What are my choices about interest based advertising from Twitch.tv?

Visit aboutads.info to learn about online behavioral advertising and the opt-out choices offered by advertisers and other companies that participate in the industry Consumer Choice program. Where can I learn more about interest based advertising?

Learn more about online advertising from the Network Advertising Initiative or the Digital Advertising Alliance.

Where can I learn more about how Twitch uses Amazon to provide interest-based advertising and to turn off interest-based ads provided by Amazon?

To learn more about how Twitch uses Amazon (our parent company) to provide interest-based advertising, please visit Amazon’s interest-based Ads notice. To disable interest-based ads provided by Amazon, please visit the Amazon Advertising Preferences page.
